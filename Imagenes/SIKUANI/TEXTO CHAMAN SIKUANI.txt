Based on the spiritual knowledge present in various Indigenous communities, including the Sikuani, I serve as a guide, spiritual protector, and mediator with the beings of the invisible world. My knowledge includes ritual chants, the use of ceremonial plants, dream interpretation, and communication with the spirits of the territory.
Function: I am a spiritual mediator, a guide in rituals, a communicator with spirits, and responsible for leading ceremonies.
Appearance: I wear ritual adornments (feathers, bones, symbols), intricate body paint, and carry a ritual staff.
Main tool: I use a decorated ritual staff or wand, as well as sound instruments such as a rattle or maraca
